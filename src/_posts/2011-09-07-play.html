--- title: Play 2.0 and exceptions layout: post ---

On May, I read Play a little and wrote:

Honestly, I don't know why Play uses an exception. It's not straightforward for me.

Then today I read a discussion about Play 2.0 on Hacker News.

  • Normally, exceptions are slow in Java because we need to generate stack trace.
  • However Play overrides fillInStackTrace to avoid the issue.
  • Then Play 2.0 doesn't use exceptions for flow control.

Well, my feeling was right.
