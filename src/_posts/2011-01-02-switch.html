--- title: Switch layout: post ---

Every year, I switch my blogware. 2009 was WordPress, 2010 was Tokyo Promenade. And now I've decided to use Jekyll.

Jekyll is a blog-aware static site generator by Tom Preston-Werner (of GitHub fame). Every post is just a file. You can manage your blog on your favorite version control system. I've pushed my repository at github.com/kzys/2011.git. If you find a typo or an error, please send an email or a pull-request.

Switch to English

I've also decided to switch my blog from Japanese to English. I'm currently reading Clay Shirky's "Cognitive Surplus". He wrote about Geocities like so:

People don't actively want bad design - it's just that most people aren't good designers, but that's not going to stop them from creating things on their own.

My English is bad, but that's not going to stop me. I want to share my code and ideas with other hackers around the world.
