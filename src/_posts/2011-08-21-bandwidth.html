Motohiro (my friend) wrote about his 30 day challenge: Stop Reading Feeds (in Japanese). After reading his post, I remembered "Expand Your Bandwidth" - a pattern from "Apprenticeship Patterns". "Expand Your Bandwidth" means read blogs, join a local user group and so on. But it has a good additional advice.

Once you understand how to Expand Your Bandwidth, the next step is to understand when to Expand Your Bandwidth.

Now I read 40 blogs. But several years ago, I read more blogs. Was I wrong? I don't think so.
